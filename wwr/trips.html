Serene River Cruise - Difficulty ★

Word from Sally

Perfect for first-timers, families, or anyone looking to soak in the scenery without breaking a sweat, our Serene River Cruise is a gentle float through lush valleys and calm waters. Birds chirp overhead, and the current does most of the work. This is nature’s version of a deep breath.

Mountain View Paddle - Difficulty: ★★

Word from Sally

With just the right touch of excitement, the Mountain View Paddle offers light rapids, wide-open views of the towering peaks, and plenty of moments to snap photos—or just take it all in. Great for adventurous beginners ready to dip a paddle into something a little livelier.

Treeline Rapids - Difficulty: ★★★

Word from Sally

This is where it all began. My grandmother, Jane River, fell into Treeline Rapids decades ago, and rather than fear it, she founded this company with a fire in her heart. This run combines swift currents, playful drops, and a few heart-thumping surprises. A true classic with a legacy you’ll feel in every splash.

Challenger Stream - Difficulty: ★★★★

Word from Sally

You want a test? Challenger Stream delivers. With fast turns, narrow channels, and non-stop action, this course is for experienced paddlers only. There’s no time for sightseeing—just teamwork, grit, and adrenaline from start to finish.

The Throngler - Difficulty: ★★★★★★★

Word from Sally

We don’t run The Throngler often—and when we do, it’s invite-only. Twisting fury, unpredictable hydraulics, and class V+ rapids make this stretch of water feared and revered. Not for the faint of heart, but for those who live for the edge. Waivers aren’t just required—they’re triple-checked.
